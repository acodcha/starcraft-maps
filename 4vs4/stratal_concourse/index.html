Stratal Concourse (4vs4)

Shared bases! Eight players compete on a medium-sized 128x128 map. The teams consist of the four northwest players versus the four southeast players. Each team features a large base shared by its four players. Each shared base features a set of sheltered rear expansions along with two sets of more exposed forward expansions. The shared bases and their forward expansions each feature wide bridges linking them to the central concourse; guarding these bridges is vital. The central concourse features additional expansions, though they are very exposed and difficult to hold.

Compatible with StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered.
