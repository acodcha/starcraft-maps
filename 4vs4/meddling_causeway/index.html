Meddling Causeway (4vs4)

Shared bases! Eight players compete on a small-sized 96x96 map. The teams consist of the four southwest players versus the four northeast players. Each team controls a large base shared by its four players. Each shared base features multiple expansions, some of which are more exposed than others. A series of bridges allow passage across the central river, though neutral structures must be destroyed to cross the bridges. Control of the bridges is key!

Compatible with StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered.
