Rustic Frontier (2vs2)

Shared bases! Four players compete on a small-sized 96x96 map. The teams consist of the two southwest players versus the two northeast players. Each team features a large high-ground base shared by its two players. The shared bases feature a sheltered rear expansion along with more exposed forward expansions on lower ground. A series of wide high-ground ridges run along the main diagonal of the map and contain additional expansions, though they are more exposed. Neutral structures must be destroyed to cross the eastern and western ridges. Control of the ridges is key!

Compatible with StarCraft: Remastered; not compatible with StarCraft: Brood War.
