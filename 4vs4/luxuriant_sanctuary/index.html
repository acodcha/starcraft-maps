Luxuriant Sanctuary (4vs4)

Eight players compete on a medium-sized 128x128 map. The teams consist of the four north players versus the four south players. Each player has their own base on high ground and access to a natural expansion on the low ground in front of their base. Additional expansions ring the map between the players' bases; though these expansions may appear sheltered, expansions that lie between enemy players are difficult to hold. A final set of expansions are scattered throughout the central sanctuary; these expansions are the most exposed, but holding the central sanctuary is strategically important as it acts as a hub to all other expansions.

Compatible with StarCraft: Remastered; not compatible with StarCraft: Brood War.
