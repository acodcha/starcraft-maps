Wintry Gully (4vs4)

Shared bases! Eight players compete on a small-sized 96x96 map. The teams consist of the four southwest players versus the four northeast players. Each team controls a large base on high ground shared by its four players. Each shared base features a set of sheltered rear expansions along with several more exposed forward expansions on low ground. A series of bridges allow passage across the central gully, though neutral structures must be destroyed to cross the bridges. Control of the bridges is key!

Compatible with StarCraft: Remastered; not compatible with StarCraft: Brood War.
