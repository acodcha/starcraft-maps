Cloistered Vale (3vs3)

Six players compete on a medium-sized 128x128 map. The teams consist of the three northwest players versus the three southeast players. Each player has their own base on high ground and access to a protected rear expansion and a more exposed forward expansion on low ground. Additional sheltered expansions ring the map between each base. The central vale is vast and overgrown with trees; controlling it provides direct access to each base.

Compatible with StarCraft: Remastered; not compatible with StarCraft: Brood War.
