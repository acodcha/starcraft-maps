This repository contains my custom-made maps for the StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered video games by Blizzard Entertainment. Maps used in professional leagues are typically designed for 1vs1 games, whereas my maps focus on 2vs2, 3vs3, and 4vs4 team games.

To play a map, simply download its .scx file and place it anywhere in your StarCraft maps directory, which is typically C:\Program Files (x86)\StarCraft\Maps\. When creating a game, select the Use Map Settings option; otherwise, player starting locations may be randomized rather than team-based.

My Battle.net BattleTag is acodcha#1294. If you see me online, drop by and say hello!
