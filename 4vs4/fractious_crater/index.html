Fractious Crater (4vs4)

Shared bases! Eight players compete on a medium-sized 128x128 map. The teams consist of the four west players versus the four east players. Each team controls a large high-ground base shared by its four players. Each shared base features multiple sheltered rear expansions and leads down into the vast central crater. The crater features numerous additional expansions, some of which are more exposed than others. Additional sheltered high-ground expansions surround the crater to its north and south. Control of the central crater is key!

Compatible with StarCraft: Remastered; not compatible with StarCraft: Brood War.
