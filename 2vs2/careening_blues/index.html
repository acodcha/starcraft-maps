Careening Blues (2vs2)

Four players are squeezed onto a tiny-sized 64x64 map! The teams consist of the two southeast players versus the two northwest players. Each team features a large base shared by its two players. Each shared base contains three expansions; one team member gets two expansions while the other gets only one, and some expansions are more exposed than others! Twin central bridges link your team's base to the opposing team's base, though neutral structures must be destroyed to cross the bridges.

Compatible with StarCraft: Remastered; not compatible with StarCraft: Brood War.
