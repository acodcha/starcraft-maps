Intertwined Platforms (2vs2)

Four players are squeezed onto a tiny-sized 64x64 map! The teams consist of the north and east players versus the south and west players. Each player's base features a protected expansion. Neutral structures must be destroyed to gain access to the central platform. The distance to your opponents' bases is very short, so be prepared for quick aggression!

Compatible with StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered.
