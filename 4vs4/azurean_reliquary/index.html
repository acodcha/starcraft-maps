Azurean Reliquary (4vs4)

Shared bases! Eight players compete on a medium-sized 128x128 map. The teams consist of the four southwest players versus the four northeast players. Each team features a large high-ground base shared by its four players. Each shared base features sheltered rear expansions along with more exposed forward expansions on lower ground. Additional expansions line the edges of the central reliquary. The reliquary is on high ground, so controlling it is key.

Compatible with StarCraft: Remastered; not compatible with StarCraft: Brood War.
